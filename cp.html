About the Company

A Muscat-based house for pioneering, AI-led work.

City Pearl for Pioneering Projects is an Omani technology company built around one idea: that intelligent software should make organizations faster to decide, easier to run, and ready for what comes next. We work at the intersection of artificial intelligence, automation and digital transformation.

Our teams combine technical depth with strategic insight, developing scalable, future-ready systems for private enterprises and government entities navigating an increasingly digital economy — grounded in Oman, built for the region.
